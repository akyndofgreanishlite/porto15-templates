2014

AdsNative Native SSP

AdsNative began as a native ad platform targeting premium publishers, enabling them to sell placements direct to advertisers. We saw an enormous opportunity to expand to a broader market, including small and mid-sized publishers, connecting them to a range of advertising partners, from brands to ad networks.

While I was there, the platform evolved into a fully featured SSP (Supply Side Platform), with the goal of always having ad content to fill a predefined number of placements on a publishers site or mobile app, including any combination of direct-sell or programmatic ads.
